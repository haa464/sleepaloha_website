Tencel Fabric (Top Layer)

Tencel fabric is an ideal choice for organic latex mattresses due to its eco-friendly properties. Derived from sustainably sourced eucalyptus wood pulp, Tencel offers exceptional breathability, moisture-wicking capabilities, and a silky-smooth texture, enhancing the overall sleep experience while promoting a healthier, more sustainable environment.

Organic Cotton

Our organic cotton is a highly desirable material for organic latex mattresses. Grown without the use of harmful chemicals and pesticides, organic cotton offers a natural and breathable surface that enhances comfort and promotes better airflow.

4-inch solid Latex Foam

The inclusion of a 4-inch latex foam layer in soft latex mattress provides exceptional comfort and support.
